CBD Care

Cannabidiol, better known as CBD, is a popular natural remedy used for many
common ailments. It is found in the cannabis plant without the pyschoactive effects,
unlike THC. This makes CBD very appealing for those looking for relief from pain and
other symptoms without the mind-altering effects of marijuana or certain pharmaceutical
drugs. CBD can be used for: pain relief, anxiety, depression and can alleviate cancer-related
symptoms, to name a few.
There are a few ways you can use CBD:
1. CBD Oil - Can be consumed in a drink or placed under your tongue before swallowing.
2. CBD Capsule - Can be swallowed for people who may not like the taste of oil.
3. CBD Vape- Can be inhaled for the quickest effect.
4. CBD Cream - Can be applied on to skin for pain or rashes.
